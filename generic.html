My Life

I was born in the historic city of Melaka, a place rich in culture and history. I am the youngest of four siblings, and being the "baby" of the family has been both a joy and a challenge. It meant growing up with strong role models to look up to and learning how to stand out in my way.

I began my schooling at SK Batu Berendam and continued my secondary education at SMK Ayer Keroh. Afterward, I pursued a Foundation in physical science at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia(UTM), where I am currently studying for my Bachelor's Degree in Data Engineering. A place of study feels like a second home to me. My school life has been an exciting journey, where I have had the opportunity to explore new subjects, participate in various activities, and make friends.
